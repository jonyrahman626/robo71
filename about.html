Welcome to the

Robo71 aims to provide a robust platform and hands-on training for innovative minds who want to make their mark in robotics. We want to take our young enthusiasts to the world of robotics where there are endless possibilities for groundbreaking innovations that are set to bring revolutionary changes to our lives. Our goal is to organize and harness the energy of promising youths throughout the country who are not being able to display their talents due to the lack of guidance. Through the quality research facilities, we want to help Bangladesh get ready for the challenges that automation is posing at us. We are committed to creating a skilled workforce for the robotics and automation sector that can compete at the global level.
