Robotics Research in Bangladesh

Robotics has gone through massive change in the recent years. Primarily invented as a helping hand in manufacturing purposes, robotâ€™s usage has been expanded to various other areas from home to deep space. Through machine learning and artificial intelligence, robots and humans are now increasingly getting into an environment of co-existing. Robotics is rapidly changing and there is still so much to achieve to fully unlock the potential. So, the importance of robotics research now bears more significance than ever. Bangladesh is moving towards becoming a digitized country. Many industries are being set up and they are in need of automation experts. But robotics education in our country is still in its infancy. Often, local expertise has not been able to live up to the market expectation due to lack of sufficient knowledge and research facilities and that forced to call up foreign help.

How We Foster Robotics Research Our Research Areas

Robo71 has taken up the initiative to engage in creating a specialized facility to meet the local and global industry needs. We are here to help anyone interested to conduct research in robotics application. Our facility encourages young thinkers to find innovative ways of robotics application that will allow robots to collaborate with humans more efficiently. We have brought in experienced local and international experts and faculties in robotics research to aid local talents. Robot enthusiasts will be given a rich institutional set up to carry out their works. Not only will they research and design for local industries but they will also be given the opportunity to work in global projects.
